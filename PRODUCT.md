# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Chess players, coaches, and followers who use ChessEver to follow live and
recent tournaments, inspect games, search players/events, and keep useful game
collections close at hand. They are usually scanning dense chess information on
mobile first, with short windows of attention during live events or study.

## Product Purpose

ChessEver brings tournament broadcasts, curated game lists, player context,
saved collections, and game analysis into one app. Success means users can find
the right game or event quickly, trust the data, and move from discovery to the
board without friction.

## Brand Personality

Focused, expert, and calm. The app should feel like a sharp chess workbench:
fast to scan, confident about hierarchy, and restrained enough that game data
stays primary.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic AI-SaaS visuals, decorative "smart" branding, busy marketing
badges, gratuitous motion, oversized novelty UI, and any treatment that makes
generated collections feel detached from the existing event-card system.

## Design Principles

- Preserve the existing product vocabulary: Inter typography, semantic
  `context.colors`, compact spacing, and cyan only for real actions or state.
- Make chess content easier to scan before adding decoration.
- Keep generated game collections native to the For You and event surfaces.
- Prefer plain, specific labels over internal implementation terms like
  "smart" or "live smart database."
- Treat mobile ergonomics as the default constraint.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Keep tap targets comfortable on
phone, support reduced-motion expectations, and avoid relying on color alone for
state or hierarchy.
