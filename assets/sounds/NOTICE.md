# Third-party sound assets

This app vendors optional Lichess Mobile sound theme assets under `assets/sounds/`.

Source: https://github.com/lichess-org/mobile
License: GNU General Public License v3.0 or later, as documented by Lichess Mobile in `COPYING.md` and `LICENSE`.
Copyright: Lichess Mobile contributors.

ChessEver's existing sound bank remains `Standard` and is stored separately under `assets/sfx/`.
The imported Lichess `standard` bank is exposed in the UI as `Lichess`; other imported banks are exposed as Piano, NES, SFX, Futuristic, and Lisp.
